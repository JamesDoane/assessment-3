Parkwood

Parkwood is a quiet neighborhood nestled in the small foothills of Keller, TX. It's filled with that classic neighborly charm, where everyone kinda knows everyone and a spur of the moment Friday night get-together is not uncommon. The houses all adhere to a cookie cutter design straight out of the 90's, but that only adds to the charm. The lawns are, for the most part, perfectly manicured and the streets are kept in relatively good condition and free of debris. The sensation one might feel by simply existing in this space could be best described as idyllic.
